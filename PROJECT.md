## The Problem

Growth marketing today requires buying and juggling multiple disconnected tools:

- **Lead Identification** — Apollo, Sales Robot, Foxx, LinkedIn scrapers
- **Lead Qualification** — Separate qualification layers / manual review
- **Data Enrichment** — Firecrawl, Clay, and similar enrichment tools
- **Decision Maker ID** — Manual research or tools like Hunter.io / Clearbit
- **Personalized Outreach** — Yet another tool for email, video, or DMs

Leads and contacts are scattered across Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, SMS, and Email — with no single source of truth. Every step requires a context switch, a new login, and a new monthly bill.

---

## The Vision

**One tool. One workflow. All platforms.**

Super Growth centralises the entire growth marketing stack into a single platform:

1. **Identify** leads across all major contact surfaces
2. **Qualify** those leads automatically using AI signals
3. **Enrich** with deep data (Firecrawl website mapping, company analysis)
4. **Identify Decision Makers** within target companies
5. **Outreach** with hyper-personalised messages, emails, or videos — at scale
